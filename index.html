last nights dream

Last night I dreamt I was breaking into a hospital. While sneaking around evading doctors and staff i found myself climbing the outside of a marble staircase. While attempting to leap from one portion to the other i became stuck and was sliding towards what would be a terrible fall. Before I met this adrenaline pumping demise however, a doctor noticed me and helped me over to the other side of the staircase railing. He looked dissapointed and just said to me "you're Elliott aren't ya?" to which I replied "why?".

The doctor spoke in an agitated yet soft tone. He asked me to follow him to a room, which i did. It was there he told me I was dying, the method, my heartrate was slowing and would eventually stop. Leaving the room, the doc informed me that there was to be a service downstairs. Who wouldn't want to go to their own funeral, or whatever pre-funeral type event this would be.

Upon arival I was in a room filled with people who knew me. Some were upset, didnt speak to me, walked past like I wasn't there. The others, well they wanted to do shots, were glad they caught me before my passing, gave hugs and talked about good times. The longer I stayed the less people were around, this wasnt some metaphor for people being close or more important in my life, in fact it seemed as though those most important dissapeared from the event soonest. Eventually there was no one left I wanted to be around, and so I left.

stuff i did yesterday

After working on this blog for a little bit and tidying up my house, I went to meet up with some friends. We ran around jumping in dumpsters and snagging a bunch of cool junk. The treasures we found furnished the barren backyard and made a pretty sick firepit.

We also gathered multiple christmas trees. With these flora, their life slowly flowing out of them akin in style with the stubborn sap from its trunk, we made a mighty fire by the river. The trees made a cold night warm, and in return we gave them the gloriously swift death they so longed for.
